## üìù Course Synopsis
---
- This foundational course introduces students to the principles of digital electronics as the basis for microprocessor-based systems in computers, robots, automobiles, and industrial control applications.
- It covers  key topics including number systems and codes, logic gates, Boolean algebra, combinational logic circuits, latches, flip-flops, counters, and shift registers, progressing from basic concepts to programmable logic devices.
- Teaching and learning are carried out through lectures, tutorials, hands-on labs with Deeds Software, and  project-based activities, emphasizing problem-solving, collaboration, and practical application.
-  By the end of  the course, students will be able to apply digital logic fundamentals, design and construct circuits using  simulation tools and real devices, and suggest improvements to enhance solutions for practical problems.
